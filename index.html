About Me

Wellcome to my website, I hope that from this website you can learn more about me. Anyways, I would describe myself as extremely motivated and focused on my study and work. I am a very serious person but if you get my dry humor I can be pretty funny too. I am trilingual and I know Russian, Hebrew, and of course English as I hope you can tell. I am quite old-fashioned as I like much older music and my dream is to one day live in New York or NJ (I am practical) so that I can visit the Met Opera and Broadway more often.
